1. OBJECTIF DU SYSTÈME

Le système de Virtual Cards Amanah permet de générer des cartes de paiement numériques instantanées, entièrement liées au wallet via le ledger.

👉 Objectif :

permettre paiements e-commerce sécurisés
réduire fraude carte physique
offrir contrôle granulaire des dépenses
activer/desactiver cartes instantanément
sécuriser paiements en ligne à grande échelle
2. CORE PRINCIPLE
2.1 Virtual card = temporary payment identity
A virtual card is a temporary, controlled payment identity linked to wallet ledger.
2.2 No independent funds
aucune valeur stockée dans la carte
tout dépend du wallet + ledger
2.3 Instant lifecycle control
création instantanée
destruction instantanée
modification instantanée des limites
3. CREATION FLOW
3.1 Step-by-step process
User requests virtual card
   ↓
Wallet validation (ledger check)
   ↓
Risk engine evaluation
   ↓
KYC status verification
   ↓
Token generation
   ↓
Virtual card issued
3.2 Constraints
max cards per user configurable
country restrictions applied
risk score threshold required
4. TYPES OF VIRTUAL CARDS
4.1 Standard Virtual Card
normal online usage
reusable until expiration
4.2 Single-Use Card
Card becomes invalid after 1 successful transaction

👉 Use case:

high-risk merchant
subscription trials
unknown websites
4.3 Merchant-Locked Card
restricted to one merchant only
prevents fraud reuse
4.4 Time-Limited Card
expires after X minutes/hours/days

7. FRAUD PREVENTION SYSTEM
7.1 Threats

7.2 Protection mechanisms

7.3 Fraud actions

8. LIMIT SYSTEM
8.1 Types of limits

8.2 Rule

9. LIFECYCLE MANAGEMENT
9.1 States
ACTIVE → SUSPENDED → FROZEN → EXPIRED → DESTROYED
9.2 Rules
expired cards cannot be reactivated
frozen cards require admin or risk unlock
destroyed cards are unrecoverable
10. REFUND SYSTEM

virtual card remains unchanged or optionally reissued
