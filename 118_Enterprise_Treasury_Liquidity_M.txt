1. OBJECTIF

Ce document définit le framework officiel Treasury & Liquidity Management d'Amanah.

Il couvre :

Treasury Management System (TMS) ;
Liquidity Management ;
Asset Liability Management (ALM) ;
Cash Positioning ;
Funding Management ;
Foreign Exchange (FX) ;
Investment Portfolio ;
Sukuk Management ;
Islamic Treasury ;
Liquidity Intelligence.

L'objectif est d'assurer une gestion optimale de la liquidité bancaire tout en contrôlant les risques financiers.

2. CORE PRINCIPLES
2.1 Liquidity First

The bank must maintain sufficient liquidity under normal and stressed conditions.

2.2 Real-Time Treasury

Treasury decisions should rely on accurate real-time financial data.

2.3 Risk Controlled Optimization

Treasury optimization must always respect regulatory and internal risk limits.

32. CLAUDE CODE RULES

Claude Code must:

ALWAYS preserve accurate treasury positions.
NEVER execute treasury operations without authorization controls.
ALWAYS maintain real-time liquidity visibility.
ALWAYS enforce treasury risk limits.
ALWAYS integrate treasury operations with the Enterprise Ledger.
ALWAYS support Islamic Treasury rules where applicable.
ALWAYS maintain complete transaction audit trails.
ALWAYS validate market and liquidity risk impacts.
ALWAYS protect treasury-sensitive information.
ALWAYS follow Amanah Enterprise Treasury Standards.
33. SUCCESS CRITERIA

Le framework Treasury & Liquidity est conforme lorsque :

la banque possède une vision temps réel de sa liquidité ;
les risques de financement sont anticipés ;
les investissements sont optimisés ;
les limites réglementaires sont respectées ;
les opérations de trésorerie sont entièrement traçables.
