1. OBJECTIF

Ce document définit le framework officiel de gestion des risques d'Amanah.

Il couvre :

Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) ;
Credit Risk ;
Market Risk ;
Liquidity Risk ;
Operational Risk ;
Counterparty Risk ;
IFRS 9 ;
Basel III / IV ;
ICAAP ;
Stress Testing ;
Capital Management.

L'objectif est d'assurer une gestion proactive, mesurable et conforme des risques auxquels la banque est exposée.

2. CORE PRINCIPLES
2.1 Risk Awareness
Risk management must be embedded in every business process.
2.2 Three Lines of Defense
Risk ownership follows the Three Lines of Defense model.
2.3 Risk-Based Decision Making
Business decisions must consider their impact on the institution's risk profile.

25. CLAUDE CODE RULES

Claude Code must:

ALWAYS evaluate financial decisions against defined risk policies.
NEVER bypass risk limits without documented approval.
ALWAYS calculate and preserve complete audit trails for risk assessments.
ALWAYS support IFRS 9 staging and Expected Credit Loss calculations.
ALWAYS enforce Basel capital and liquidity constraints where applicable.
ALWAYS separate model configuration from business logic.
ALWAYS support stress testing and scenario analysis.
ALWAYS version risk models and assumptions.
ALWAYS integrate risk controls with operational workflows.
ALWAYS follow Enterprise Risk Management Standards.
26. SUCCESS CRITERIA

Le framework de gestion des risques est conforme lorsque :

tous les risques matériels sont identifiés et suivis ;
les limites sont contrôlées automatiquement ;
les calculs IFRS 9 et les indicateurs Bâle sont reproductibles ;
les modèles sont gouvernés et auditables ;
les décisions de risque sont documentées et traçables.
