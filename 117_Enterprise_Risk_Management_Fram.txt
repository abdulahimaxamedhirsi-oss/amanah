2. CORE PRINCIPLES
2.1 Risk Awareness

Every business decision must consider risk impact.

2.2 Risk-Based Management

Resources and controls must be aligned with risk exposure.

2.3 Forward Looking Risk

Risk management must anticipate future threats, not only react to past events.

32. CLAUDE CODE RULES

Claude Code must:

ALWAYS evaluate risk impact when designing financial capabilities.
NEVER bypass risk controls or exposure limits.
ALWAYS maintain risk calculation audit trails.
ALWAYS support configurable risk thresholds.
ALWAYS integrate risk monitoring into financial workflows.
ALWAYS validate AI models used for risk decisions.
ALWAYS support stress testing capabilities.
ALWAYS protect sensitive risk information.
ALWAYS maintain separation between risk approval and execution.
ALWAYS follow Amanah Enterprise Risk Management Standards.
33. SUCCESS CRITERIA

Le framework Risk Management est conforme lorsque :

les risques sont identifiés avant impact majeur ;
les limites de risque sont surveillées en temps réel ;
les décisions sont basées sur des données fiables ;
les modèles sont contrôlés et validés ;
la direction possède une vision complète de l'exposition bancaire.
